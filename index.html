LXD hosting nginx

Replacing FreeNAS and hosting, March 19, 2018

Recently there were some storms that passed through and my FreeNAS was
having trouble restarting. Eventually I realized it was the USB that
my Dell R710 was booting from, but by then I had migrated to a USB
to sata cable with a samsung evo 850 ssd with ubuntu 16.04 server on it.

1. Setup mdadm my RAID, find your devices with this command.
lsblk -o NAME,SIZE,FSTYPE,TYPE,MOUNTPOINT

2. Create RAID 10 with desired drive
sudo mdadm --create --verbose /dev/md0 --level=10 /
--raid-devices=4 /dev/sdb /dev/sdc /dev/sdd /dev/sde

3. View RAID device creation status
cat /proc/mdstat

4 .Using watch to get 2 second interval updates
watch cat /proc/mdstat

5. Create ext4 filesystem on device
sudo mkfs.ext4 -F /dev/md0

6. Create directory mount it the new volume for access
mkdir -p /mnt/md0
mount /dev/md0 /mnt/md0
df -h -x devtmpfs -x tmpfs

7. Auto mount RAID volume
mdadm --detail --scan | sudo tee -a /etc/mdadm/mdadm.conf
sudo update-initramfs -u
echo 'dev/md0 /mnt/md0 ext4 defaults,nofail,discard 0 0' | sudo tee -a /etc/fstab
/dev/md0 /mnt/md0 ext4 defaults,nofail,discard 0 0

Mead staggard nutrient fermentation

Optomizing yeast and time, July 15, 2017

Requirements Sanitizer spray bottle, 1 gallon carboy, funnel, 3 lbs honey, teaspoon.
Start yeast - sterilize bowl 2oz water @ 104-109 f for 15 minutes
Fill carboy 1/3 with water, add honey and stir vigorously.
Note : Avoid heating the honey for better flavor or effect.
Potable water probably brewable water.
Add yeast to carboy fill with water until 3 inches left.
Fit airlock with sterile water.
Staggard Nutrient Bonus rounds!
Add yeast nutrients on the following days after initial creation.

Rack after clear, approximately 2 - 3 weeks.
