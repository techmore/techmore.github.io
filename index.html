Tailwind Oatmeal & The Incus Pivot

Upgrading aesthetics and infrastructure, January 9, 2026

Today marks a significant shift for the Techmore stack. We've officially migrated our design system to a custom Tailwind "Oatmeal" Olive palette, leveraging the precision of OKLCH colors for a more sophisticated, premium feel. This entire redesign was orchestrated via OpenCode, allowing for rapid, programmatic UI iterations directly on GitHub.

On the infrastructure side, we've made the decision to transition from LXD to Incus. As the community-driven fork of LXD, Incus aligns better with our commitment to open-source tooling and provides a more robust foundation for our containerized services. The transition was seamless, and the performance gains are already noticeable.

Meanwhile, the labs are buzzing with the early previews of FreeBSD 15 (Sylve). The architectural refinements in this upcoming release look incredibly promising, especially for high-density networking tasks. It's an exciting time to be rolling boulders.
