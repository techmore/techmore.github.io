UniFi Switch Comparison Under $1000

2.5GbE focus with options for every need, March 4, 2026

After picking your gateway (see our UniFi Firewall Comparison), you'll need a switch. This guide covers UniFi switches under $1000 with a focus on 2.5GbE options for home and small business use.

2.5GbE is the sweet spot: Most new devices (PCs, NAS, gaming consoles) support 2.5GbE. It's 2.5x faster than gigabit, at only a small premium. We recommend 2.5GbE as the minimum for any new build.

Category 1: 2.5GbE Switches (Recommended)

The modern standard. 2.5GbE gives you 2.5x gigabit speeds without the premium of 10GbE. Perfect for homes and small offices.

UniFi Flex Mini 2.5G

$59

5 ports • 2.5GbE each • Fanless • Desktop

Use this if: You want the cheapest way to get 2.5GbE. It's tiny, fanless (silent), and powered by USB-C. Great for a desktop next to your router or as a small office switch. No PoE.

UniFi Flex 2.5G PoE ⭐ BEST VALUE

$199

8 ports • 2.5GbE • PoE++ (46W budget) • 10G uplink

Use this if: You want the best price/performance switch UniFi makes. 8 ports of 2.5GbE with PoE for APs or cameras. The 10G SFP+ uplink future-proofs your setup. Fanless and versatile mounting. Our top pick.

UniFi Ultra 60W

$159

8 ports • 1GbE + 2.5GbE • PoE+ (60W budget)

Use this if: You need PoE but want a lower-cost alternative to the Flex 2.5G PoE. Has 2 dedicated 2.5GbE ports + 6 gigabit. Good for powering a UniFi AP + a few cameras.

UniFi Enterprise 8 PoE

$479

8 ports • 2.5GbE • PoE+ (120W budget) • 2x 10G SFP+

Use this if: You need more PoE power (120W) and want proper SFP+ fiber uplinks. It's the "pro" version of the Flex 2.5G PoE with more power, Layer 2+ features, and a metal chassis. Good for small business.

UniFi Pro Max 16 PoE

$549

16 ports • 2.5GbE + 10GbE • PoE++ (400W budget) • 2x 25G SFP28

Use this if: You need 16 ports with serious power. 400W PoE budget can run 8+ UniFi U6-Pro APs or multiple cameras. The 25G SFP28 ports are for stacking or high-speed fiber. Enterprise-grade.

Recommendation for 2.5GbE:

  • Budget: Flex Mini 2.5G ($59) - just add ports
  • Best value (our pick): Flex 2.5G PoE ($199) - 8 ports, PoE, 10G uplink
  • More PoE power: Enterprise 8 PoE ($479) - 120W budget
  • 16 ports: Pro Max 16 PoE ($549) - 400W PoE++

Category 2: Gigabit (Budget) Switches

Still useful for IoT networks, guest networks, or when budget is tight. 1GbE is plenty for basic devices.

UniFi Flex Mini

$29

5 ports • 1GbE • Fanless • Desktop

Use this if: You want the cheapest UniFi switch. Good for extending a network to another room, or for IoT VLANs where you don't need speed. USB-C powered.

UniFi Lite 8 PoE

$109

8 ports • 1GbE • PoE+ (52W budget)

Use this if: You need PoE on a budget. Can power 1-2 UniFi APs or a few cameras. The budget is limited so don't plan on full PoE++ devices.

UniFi Lite 16 PoE

$179

16 ports • 1GbE • PoE+ (150W budget) • 2x 1G SFP

Use this if: You need 16 ports with PoE but don't need 2.5GbE. Good for larger deployments of older devices or when cost is the main factor.

Recommendation for Gigabit:

  • Cheapest: Flex Mini ($29) - basic expansion
  • Budget PoE: Lite 8 PoE ($109) - small deployments
  • 16 port budget: Lite 16 PoE ($179)

Category 3: 10GbE (Pro/Future-Proof)

For prosumers, home labs, or anyone with 10GbE devices (fast NAS, editing workstations). Higher cost but maximum speed.

UniFi Flex XG

$249

12 ports • 10GbE • PoE++ (180W budget)

Use this if: You want 10GbE at a reasonable price. The most affordable 10GbE UniFi switch. Has 4 dedicated 10G ports + 8 RJ45 that can do 2.5GbE. Great for home labs.

UniFi Pro XG 8 PoE

$499

8 ports • 10GbE • PoE++ (185W budget) • 2x 10G SFP+

Use this if: You need full 10GbE with PoE. Each port is 10GbE, can power UniFi U7 Pro Max APs. The "pro" version has better PoE budget and build quality than the Flex XG.

Recommendation for 10GbE:

  • Best value 10GbE: Flex XG ($249) - entry to 10G
  • 10GbE + PoE: Pro XG 8 PoE ($499) - pro features

Building Your Network

Most home networks follow a simple pattern: Gateway → Switch → Devices. Here's how to combine:

🏠 Typical Home Network

UCG-Max → Flex 2.5G PoE → UniFi APs + Cameras + Computers

🏠 Home + Cameras

UCG-Max → Flex 2.5G PoE → (4) G5 cameras + (2) U6-Pro APs

🏢 Small Office

UDM-Pro → Pro Max 16 PoE → Multiple APs + Many cameras + Workstations

🧪 Home Lab

UDM-Pro → Pro XG 8 PoE → 10G NAS + Editing Workstation + Servers

Final Verdict

  • 🏆 Best switch for most homes: Flex 2.5G PoE ($199) - 8 ports, 2.5GbE, PoE, 10G uplink. The sweet spot.
  • 💰 Budget pick: Flex Mini 2.5G ($59) - cheapest 2.5GbE, just add ports.
  • 🚀 Best for 10GbE: Flex XG ($249) - entry to 10G with PoE.
  • 📦 Best 16-port: Lite 16 PoE ($179) - budget option, or Pro Max 16 PoE ($549) for 2.5GbE.
  • 🔌 Most PoE power: Pro Max 16 PoE ($549) - 400W budget for large deployments.

Note: Prices and availability fluctuate. Check store.ui.com for current pricing. All UniFi switches require a UniFi Gateway or Cloud Key to manage - they don't have a built-in controller.
